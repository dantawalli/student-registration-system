Student Registration System

The Student Registration System is a digital platform designed to streamline the registration process for students.

It allows students to input and manage their personal details, including name, class, address, and contact number.

The system features intuitive functionalities for editing and deleting records, ensuring accurate and up-to-date student information.

This user-friendly system enhances administrative efficiency by simplifying data entry and management tasks.
